About

Welcome to the website for Computer Vision 2! This course is designed to provide you with a deeper understanding of computer vision techniques and their practical applications. Over the course of four lectures, we will cover a range of topics from projective geometry and camera models to dense 3D reconstruction.

In addition to the lectures, we are excited to offer a new experimental session in this course, which provides an opportunity for students to gain hands-on experience with various cutting-edge computer vision topics. This session will give you a chance to apply the concepts and techniques learned in the lectures to real-world problems and explore new areas of computer vision.

The class is taught by Prof. Theo Gevers, Assistant Prof. Martin Oswald, Dr. Sezer Karaoglu. The teaching assistant team consists of Qi Bi, Melis Öcal, Pieter Pierrot, Ozzy Ülger, Ronny Velastegui Sandoval, Weijie Wei, Xiaoyan Xing.
